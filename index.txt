Python 
------

General purpose programming language. Guido Van Rossum. 1991

High level, Dynamic, Object oriented, Cross-platform
Can be used at all phases of software life cycle. 
Analyze, design, prototype, code, test, debug, tune, document, maintain. 

One of the design goals: Play well with others (Python being part of a solution)

Very High level language, higher level of abstraction (Python, JavaScript, Ruby, Perl)

Cython; Ctypes; CEFI
